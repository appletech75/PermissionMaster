Welcome To the PermissionMaster Website

PermissionMaster is a macOS app that simplifies the management of app permissions on your system. With an intuitive interface, it allows you to easily grant or revoke access to resources like microphone, camera, location (coming soon), and more for individual applications. Stay tuned for updates.
